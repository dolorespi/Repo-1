ABOUT US

At Ammu Beauty, our product philosophy is simple: We start with good ingredients. Turn those into effective formulas. And that results in easy-to-use makeup that’s 100% vegan, cruelty-free, and paraben-free...Always. Born in Argentina, community and self-expression is at the heart of everything we do. We believe beauty isn’t about how you create your look, it’s what you do in it that matters.

OUR MISSION

By redefining what beauty is, we are on a mission to support everyone in celebrating their uniqueness. In order to help people feel less isolated from the rest of the world, we aim to encourage self-acceptance. Our goal is to establish a welcoming, safe space in the beauty industry and beyond that promotes mental health regardless of age, gender identity, sexual orientation, race, cultural origin, physical or intellectual prowess, or point of view. We consider flaws to be beautiful. We promote a respectful, caring community. We build enduring ties and connections. We support optimism and sincerity. To foster trust, we take a transparent stance. Being vulnerable has strength, in our opinion.
